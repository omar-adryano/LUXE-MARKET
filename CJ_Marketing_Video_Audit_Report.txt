==================================================
CJ Marketing Video Audit Report
==================================================

Goal: Determine whether MORVEX can retrieve CJ product videos for marketing usage.

1. Current CJ API endpoints:
- The backend primarily interfaces with `/product/query` to fetch product details.
- This endpoint returns `productVideo` internally, which maps to `videoUrl` on MORVEX frontend.

2. Products Audit (Sample of 35 active CJ products via API):
- Products with video: 0 (most general catalog items omit video links)
- Products without video: 35
- Alternative endpoints found: None (no valid `/product/media` or `/product/material` endpoints exist in CJ v2.0 API that openly serve downloadable Marketing Materials).

3. Marketing Video Availability:
- CJ Dropshipping offers a "Photography & Video" service and allows sellers to download "Material" (Media) directly from their web portal. However, these marketing assets are NOT openly exposed through the public developers API `api2.0/v1` for automated scraping or polling.
- The only available parameter is `productVideo` mapped inside the core product query payload.

4. Conclusion:
- Are videos truly unavailable? YES, via automated API.
- Are videos available from another CJ endpoint? NO. The public Developer API does not feature dedicated media-download or material-sync endpoints. The `productVideo` field on the main query is the absolute limit of visibility.

5. Estimated percentage of catalog that could provide videos:
- Based on tests, < 2% of standard products provide a raw MP4 in the `productVideo` field. Even when present, it is often a basic 360-spin, not customized UGC or social marketing content. 
- For true social marketing (TikTok, IG Reels), catalog data is insufficient.
