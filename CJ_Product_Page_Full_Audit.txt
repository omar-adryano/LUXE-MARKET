SECTION 1
Current MORVEX Product Page Score
==================================================

Layout Structure: 7/10
- 12-col grid (6 cols gallery, 6 cols config) is modern and clean.
- Visually appealing but lacks the functional depth required for drop-shipping conversions.

Gallery & Video Behavior: 6/10
- Supports video conditionally (activeThumbnail check).
- Uses horizontal scrolling for thumbnails.
- Video displaces standard images but lacks elegant inline playback hooks standard on CJ.

Variant Selectors: 5/10
- Disconnected from variant-specific images. Clicking "Color" doesn't switch the main picture.
- Doesn't reflect variant-specific price changes or real SKU logic.

Quantity Selector: 8/10
- Standard and functional. Sticky bar placement on mobile is strong.

Shipping & Delivery Estimate: 0/10
- COMPLETELY MISSING. Just a static string: "Shipping calculated at checkout."
- No country selector on the product page.
- No delivery estimation window (e.g., 7-15 days).

Weight System: 0/10
- MISSING ON FRONTEND. The `weight` field exists in the Mongoose schema but is not exposed in `src/types.ts` or rendered in the UI. Total weight is not calculated.

Mobile Responsiveness: 7/10
- Stacks correctly, but the fixed bottom bar introduces vertical clutter because Add to Cart, Qty, Wishlist, and Buy Now stack heavily on smaller displays.

Overall Rating: 4.7 / 10

==================================================
SECTION 2
Missing Features Compared To CJ
==================================================

1. On-Page Shipping & Delivery Calculator
   CJ allows users to select "Ship to [Country]" on the product page, instantly calculating cost and estimated ETA.

2. Dynamic Weight Visibility
   CJ shows standard item weight (e.g., 150g). Users need to see shipment weight calculations.

3. Variant Image Syncing
   Selecting an option on CJ jumps the gallery to the correct variant photo.

4. Warehouse Selection
   "Dispatch From: US / China" is missing.

5. Real Inventory Stock
   CJ shows exact component stock. MORVEX lacks visual warnings for low stock.

SECTION 3
Implementation Roadmap
==================================================

Priority 1
Critical Revenue Improvements
- Design and integrate an "On-Page Shipping Calculator" above the Add To Cart button.
- Add a Country dropdown that queries `/api/shipping/calculate` dynamically.
- Display "Estimated Delivery: X - Y days" based on CJ Shipping Cache data to boost conversion trust.

Priority 2
Weight + Shipping Improvements
- Add `weight` to `Product` interface in `src/types.ts`.
- Build a Weight display: `Base Weight: {weight}g | Total Dispatch Weight: {weight * quantity}g`.
- Refactor the shipping calculation endpoint to intelligently scale by weight rather than a linear `cost * quantity` multiplier (if the API supports tiered weights).

Priority 3
Video Improvements
- Optimize the video player to auto-play silently on scroll/hover.
- Render the video natively in a modal or PiP (Picture-in-Picture) rather than fully replacing the primary product image to keep visual flow intact.

Priority 4
Mobile Improvements
- Optimize the fixed bottom bar (`fixed bottom-0`). Ensure it doesn't overlap essential shipping info.
- Align "Add To Cart" and "Secure Checkout" on mobile to shrink visually or group them dynamically so they consume less vertical viewport space (e.g., horizontal grid for buttons).
